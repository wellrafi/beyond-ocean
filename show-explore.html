Havelock

Havelock Island is the precious gem of Andaman with beautiful sandy beaches and lush green forest. This natural paradise is a perfect blend of white sandy beaches, coral reefs boosting colourful array of aquatic life, palm trees, and dense forests. Havelock is a longish piece of land lying on a north-west to south-east axis in the group of islands collectively known as 'Ritchie's Archipelago'. This is one of the most sought after honeymoon destination in India.

The island is so full of life that it will make you forget all your worries and help you rediscover yourself.

There is so much to do here; you can go camping by the beach or kayaking through the mangrove or exploring the aquatic world. This island has some of the best diving sites, which will just make you fall in love with this place.

Beaches

Radhanagar

Radhanagar is a two kilometer long, crescent-shaped beach framed by fine, mature specimen trees of the tropical rain-forest. It stretches to the north and south by steeply rising ground which becomes forest covered buffs dropping down to the sea and the mangrove trees. For those who like to be with the nature, there are cozy and eco-friendly accommodation right on the beach. Do stay there till sunset as the picture- perfect view will remain etched in your mind for the rest of your life.
