Day 2 · Cellular Jail

Arrival in Port Blair

Upon your arrival in Port Blair in the morning/afternoon, meet our tour representative who assists you in smooth transfer to the hotel in Port Blair. On arrival, check in at the hotel. Rest for a while. Then starts the breath-taking trip to Carbyn’s Cove Beach – One of the most picturesque, coconut palms fringed Beach, 7 kms away from Port Blair town. After this, you visit the extremely popular Cellular Jail. Formerly known as Kala Pani, it was a colonial prison used by the British to deportee political prisoners to this remote location. The evening is scheduled for enjoying the famous Light and Sound Show at Cellular Jail. The show speaks volumes about the saga of countless martyrs of Indian Independence struggle who laid down their lives. Overnight stay is at Port Blair
